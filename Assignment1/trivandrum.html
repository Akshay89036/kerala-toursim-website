Top 4 must-visit destinations in Trivandrum
Trivandrum or Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala State in the south of India.
It is known for its calm beaches, backwaters, hill stations, and rich culture because of which it attracts several Indian as well as foreign tourists. It is one of the cleanest and calm cities in India.
